How To Play

1. Create 2–10 teams.

2. Each team chooses 3 power-ups.

3. Each team chooses 3 categories.

4. Duplicate categories are not allowed.

5. All selected categories create one shared board.

6. Teams take turns choosing questions.

7. The active team locks their answer first.

8. Other teams may answer, but cannot use a locked answer.

9. Any team with the correct answer earns the points.

10. Highest score wins.
